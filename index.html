About FCA

Faculty of Computer Application


The Faculty of Computer Applications (FCA) is a dedicated department within AITR that focuses on providing comprehensive academic programs in the field of computer applications. The department typically offers courses at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, including Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA), and potentially other specialized programs like Integrated MCA (IMCA).

Our Vision


At the Faculty of Computer Applications (FCA), our vision is to be a leading institution that empowers students to become future innovators and leaders in the dynamic field of computer applications. We aspire to create a learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and technical excellence, enabling our graduates to make significant contributions to the ever-evolving world of technology.

Our Mission


Comprehensive Education: Deliver high-quality programs to equip students with a robust foundation in computer applications, preparing them for professional success.

Innovation and Collaboration: Cultivate a culture of innovation and research, motivating students and faculty to explore cutting-edge technologies and contribute to advancements in the field through active industry collaborations.

Holistic Development: Focus on nurturing students' holistic development, fostering interpersonal skills, leadership qualities, and ethical values to shape responsible and well-rounded individuals engaged in lifelong learning.

Courses

Bachelor of Computer Applications, it is an undergraduate academic degree program that focuses on computer applications and software development. BCA is a popular choice for students who are interested in pursuing a career in the field of information technology and computer science.
Master of Computer Applications, it is a postgraduate academic degree program that builds on the foundational knowledge gained in undergraduate studies, particularly in the field of computer applications and information technology.
Integrated Master of Computer Applications (IMCA) is a unique and specialized academic program that combines both undergraduate and postgraduate studies in computer applications. This integrated program typically spans a duration of five years and is designed to provide students with a seamless educational journey from the basics of computer science to advanced and specialized topics.
